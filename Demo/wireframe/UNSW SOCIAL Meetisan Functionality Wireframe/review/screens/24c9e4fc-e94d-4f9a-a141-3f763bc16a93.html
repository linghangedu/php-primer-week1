Page Description:
This page shows a list of People or Meetings.

Everything seen on this page is filitered through Tags (top right button) and the Search Distance (top left button).

Interactions:
> Press "Meetings" to show a list of meetings
     > Press "Closest" to show a list of meetings that are Closest to you
     > Press "About to Occur" to show a list of meetings that are About to Occur

> Press "People" to show a list of people
     > Press "Common Tags" to sort view of People according to Common Tags
     > Press "Closest" to sort view of People according to how close they are to you

> Press the top left menu button to open options
     > Press the "Search Distance' box to select how far the app searches for People and Meetings around you
     > Press "Who's Around" to show ALL people within the search distance (i.e. not just limited to tags)
     > Press "What's New" to show ALL meetings that are occuring within the search distance

> Press the top right tag button to edit the Tag Filter
