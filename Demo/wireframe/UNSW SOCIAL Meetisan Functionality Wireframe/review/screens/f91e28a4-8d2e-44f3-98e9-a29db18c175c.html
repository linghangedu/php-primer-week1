Page Description:
This page shows a list of Meeting Details.

Interactions:
> Press "X Attendees" to show a list of the people who attended the meeting
> Press "Meet" to join the meeting
> Press the "<" to go back to the previous page
> Press the "..." to open a menu

Information
All this information should be automatically pulled from the creator's meeting information
> 'Meeting Title' is the title of the meeting
> 'Meeting Tags' shows the tags of the meeting
> 'Meeting Description' shows the meeting description
> 'Time - Time' shows the timeframe of the meeting e.g. 10am - 11am
> 'Day, Date Month' shows the date of the meeting e.g. Tue, 7 Feb
> 'Meeting Location' shows the  the described location of the meeting
> 'Address' shows the exact address of the meeting
     > A map should also show the address
> 'How to find the attendees/s' should show information on how find the meeting
> 'Meeting Privacy' should show whether the event is Private or Public

> "X Attendees" should should the number of attendees of meeting e.g. 3 attendees
