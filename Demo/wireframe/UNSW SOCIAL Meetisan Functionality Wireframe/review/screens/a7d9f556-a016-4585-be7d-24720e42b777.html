Page Description:
This page shows a list of Meetings the user has attended/created

Everything seen on this page is filitered through Tags (top right button) and three tab filters - Alphabetical, Common Tags, Chronological

Interactions:
> Press "Alphabetical" to view Meetings in alphabetical order
> Press "Common Tags" (default view) to view Meetings according to Common Tags
> Press "Chronological" to view Meetings according to how recently the user met them

> Press the tag button at the top right to view a Tag Filter

> Press a Meeting to view its details
