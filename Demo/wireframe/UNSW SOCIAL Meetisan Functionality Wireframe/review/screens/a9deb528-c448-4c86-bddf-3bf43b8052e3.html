Page Description:
This page shows a list of Connections. Connections are People that the user has met through the app.

Everything seen on this page is filitered through Tags (top right button) and the middle three tabs - Alphabetical, Common Tags, or Chronological.

Interactions:
> Press "Alphabetical" to view Connections in alphabetical order
> Press "Common Tags" (default view) to view Connections according to Common Tags
> Press "Chronological" to view Connections according to how recently the user met them

> Press the tag button at the top right to view a Tag Filter

> Press a Connection to view their profile
